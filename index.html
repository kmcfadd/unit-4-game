Chaos Emerald Collector


Welcome the the Chaos Emerald Collector! The game starts by giving you a random number.

The emeralds below will add points to your total every time you click one.

Your job is to match the value of the random number by clicking the emeralds. Get it exact and you win, but beware, if you go over the random number then you lose!

You won't know the value of each emerald until you click on them once.

Every time the game starts over, the emeralds values with be randomized. Good luck!
